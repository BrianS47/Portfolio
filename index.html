My name is Brian Simmons.

I am a full-time US Government contractor and an aspiring software engineer. I am not afraid to take risks and try new things even if there is a high chance for failure.

I am a natural analyst and logical thinker, so I view every problem as solveable given the right steps and actions taken.

About Me

I'm obssesed with the creative process, that is what draws me in to programming. Unlike other mediums of creation, whether it be woodworking or painting, software has no ceiling. With software you can create anything your mind can imagine without worrying about physical materials or medium limitations.

The aspect of my personality that I think makes me effective on any team I join, whether it is my squad in the US Army or a team of software developers, is being able to cogently communicate ideas to others. That quality goes a long way not just for the sake of a product but also maintaining morale in your team as well.
